Chien-Fu Cheng received the Ph.D. degree in computer science from National Chiao-Tung University, Hsinchu, Taiwan, in 2008. He joined the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Tamkang University, Taiwan, in 2009 and was promopted to professor in 2017. Since 2021, he joined the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, National Taiwan Ocean University, Taiwan.

Dr. Cheng published many papers in several prestigious conferences and journals, such as IEEE LCN, IEEE MASS, IEEE VTC, IEEE WCNC, IEEE Trans. Autom. Sci. Eng., IEEE Trans. Intell. Transp. Syst., IEEE Trans. Mobile Comput., IEEE Trans. Veh. Technol., IEEE Internet Things J., IEEE Sensors J., and IEEE Commun. Lett., J. Netw. Comput. Appl, Comput. Netw., Comput. Commun., Pervasive Mob. Comput., and J. Supercomput. Dr. Cheng served as a TPC Member of more than 100 communication and computer conferences, such as IEEE GLOBECOM, IEEE ICC, IEEE INFOCOM, and IEEE WCNC. He is currently an associate editor for the IEEE Access, Springer Discover Sensors and the Elsevier Heliyon.

His current research interests include internet of things, cloud computing, wireless communication and mobile computing, wireless ad hoc and sensor networks, distributed computing and fault tolerant computing.
